Home Visit

In this pandemic, it can be difficult for most people to step out of their homes for blood check-ups and for others, it can be risky. At Metropolis, we understand that health cannot take a backseat even if there’s a COVID-19 outbreak going on. Our medically equipped and trained crew members can offer dependable blood collection at the patient’s convenience at his home or office. Good inner health in the safety of your home. Book a home collection service & get tested.

Blood Testing

A blood test checkup is the single most important test you can get to prevent, diagnose and manage a wide variety of diseases and conditions. Blood tests can help you and your doctor learn more about your health. Metropolis offers safe and Hygienic service for all your blood test needs. Today we conduct over 30 million tests annually with accuracy each year. Our repertoire extends over 4500 tests and over 100 different technologies. Quick and Accurate reports with 3 Level validations.

Covid Testing

Metropolis has been approved by the Government of India to test and collect samples of COVID-19 under ICMR Approved COVID-19 testing registration no. Metro 001(Mumbai), MC-2518 (Chennai), MHLNDD (Delhi). Home Sample Collection is available for COVID-19 test, which is a swab based corona test. We offer safe and hygienic sample collection at home by our trained technicians. Get accurate and quick reports done in 24-48 hours*.

Online Report

It is easy and convenient for patients to check their lab test results online with just a couple of clicks. Now, Get your test report in Whatsapp, Mail or SMS according to your comfort. Patients who are sick or away from the healthcare centre will no longer have to come to collect their reports, they can now access the report online with ease of navigation and quicker accessibility to information.
