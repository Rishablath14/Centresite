At Metropolis, we understand that health cannot take a backseat even if there’s a COVID-19 outbreak going on. Blood Testing

Blood tests show whether the levels of different substances in your blood fall within a normal range.Blood tests can help you and your doctor learn more about your health. Metropolis offers safe and Hygienic service for all your blood test needs. Today we conduct over 30 million tests annually with accuracy each year. Our repertoire extends over 4500 tests and over 100 different technologies. Quick and Accurate reports with 3 Level validations.

Covid Testing

Metropolis has been approved by the Government of India to test and collect samples of COVID-19 under ICMR Approved COVID-19 testing registration no. Metro 001(Mumbai), MC-2518 (Chennai), MHLNDD (Delhi). Home Sample Collection is available for COVID-19 test, which is a swab based corona test. We offer safe and hygienic sample collection at home by our trained technicians. Get accurate and quick reports done in 24-48 hours*.

Online Report
